Deidra Morrison Wells

Hi !

I'm Deidra.. Ph.D. in Computer Science with experience teaching and researching in software development and user design. My areas of research are Human Centered Computing, with a specific focus on social computing to include examinations of relationship maintenance strategies through digital technologies and the area of Cybersecurity. I focus on human factors that influence the degree of success of various attack vectors. Consistent with my experience in evaluating system usability, I participate in user centered technology development for behavioral health, and research underrepresentation in behavioral and public health investigation. Additional skills include full stack web application development. I provide these services for clients using front end development tools as well as perform backend scripting and database configuration for simple functionality.
